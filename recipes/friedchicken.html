Buttermilk Fried Chicken

Description

A simple and easy to do buttermilk fried chicken!

Ingredients

  • One pound chicken cut into 8 pieces
  • Teaspoon of black pepper
  • 2 Teaspoons of salt
  • Teaspoon of paprika
  • Half teaspoon of white pepper
  • Quarter teaspoon of dried rosemary
  • Quarter teaspon of ground thyme
  • Quarter teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Quarter teaspoon of dried sage
  • Half teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 2 cups of buttermilk
  • 2 cups of flour
  • Peanut or canola oil for frying
  • Onion and garlic powder
Steps
  1. Combine all the flour, half the salt, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, white pepper and onion powder in a large dish
  2. Toss the chicken pieces in black pepper, salt, paprika, white pepper, rosemary, thyme, oregano, sage, and cayenne pepper in a bowl
  3. Stir in buttermilk and refrigerate for 6 hours
  4. Remove chicken from buttermilk and dredge each piece in seasoned flour; shake off excess and transfer to a plate
  5. Heat oil in a large dutch oven to 350 F
  6. Fry each piece for 10 minutes, turn and fry it again for 10 minutes
  7. Transfer the cooked chicken to a cooling rack and serve after 10 minutes
