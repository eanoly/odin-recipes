Creamy Potato Salad

Description

Worlds best potato salad that everyone enjoys!

Ingredients

  • 6 medium potatoes
  • Half cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs beaten
  • salt to taste
  • 1 can of evaporated milk
  • half a cup of vinegar
  • teaspoon of yellow mustard
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1 cup of mayo
  • 6 large eggs hard cooked
  • small onion and celery, chopped
  • teaspoon of cornstarch
Steps
  1. Cook the potatoes in a boiling water until tender, drain and set aside
  2. Whisk together eggs, sugar, cornstarch and salt
  3. Stir in milk, vinegar, and mustard. Cook on medium heat for about 10 minutes until thickened.
  4. Cool it and add in mayo later
  5. Peel and dice potatoes, eggs, onions, and celery and mix
  6. Stir the dressing in gently
  7. Refrigerate before serving
