Sweet and Easy Corn

Description

Sweet and Easy corn for everyone!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 6 ears corn on the cob, husks and silk removed
Steps
  1. Fill a large pot with water and bring it to a boil
  2. Stir in sugar and lemon juice until it is dissolved.
  3. Gently place the ears of corn in boiling water, cover the pot, turn off the heat, and let it cook for 10 minutes until tender.
  4. Serve right away!
